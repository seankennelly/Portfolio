Seán Kennelly

Web Developer

Having worked in live music for years, I decided on a career move into Web Development. After studying in Australia, I moved back to the UK to continue my growth as a developer. My project experience so far has seen me launch seven websites (including this one). I'm inspired by diverse design, exciting digital experiences, and inclusive products. In my free time I'm usually working on my latest album of rowdy music.
